Flower pizza

This is a recipe that is extremely versatile, it would sattisfy everyone, from the pickiest to the biggest gourmets. Feel free to make it your own.

Some of the ingredients we would recommend to try on it are: peppers, onions, mushrooms, olives, bacon, prosciutto, mozzarella, cheddar, feta, gorgonzola, and many more.

  • 40g fresh yeast
  • 1/2 tsp. sugar
  • 200ml warm milk
  • 600g flour
  • 1 tsp. baking powder
  • 40g butter/olive oil
  • 2 large eggs
  • 200ml yogurt
  • 500ml tomato sauce
  • 250g ham/pepperoni
  • 500g cheese
  • 30g parmigiano
  1. In a small bowl, combine warm milk, sugar, and fresh yeast. Leave it aside to rise.
  2. In the meantime, sift the flour and baking powder into a large bowl. Add a pinch of salt.
  3. Once the yeast mixture has risen, add melted butter (or olive oil), eggs, yogurt, and the yeast mixture into the flour. Mix and knead the dough thoroughly until it becomes compact and smooth. If the dough is sticky, you can gradually add more flour.
  4. Cover the dough and let it rest in a warm spot for an hour or until it roughly doubles in size.
  5. After the dough has risen, transfer it onto a clean surface. Use a rolling pin to stretch the dough to a thickness of about 0.5cm.
  6. Take a large round cookie cutter to cut out dough circles. If you don't have a cookie cutter, a regular round glass can be used as an alternative.
  7. Cut as many dough circles as needed, and the remaining dough can be kneaded, rolled out, and cut again.
  8. Take a dough disc in your hand and add tomato sauce, ham (or pepperoni), and cheese as desired. Fold the disc in half and twist it around itself to create a petal shape.
  9. Place the shaped petals into a round baking tray, starting from the center and adding more petals around the center ones until the tray is filled.
  10. Sprinkle the flower pizza with parmigiano cheese.
  11. Preheat the oven to 180ºC (350ºF) and bake the flower pizza for approximately 25 minutes.
  12. Once the pizza is done, remove it from the oven and let it rest for 5-10 minutes.
  13. Serve the flower-shaped pizza on a large plate or serving platter. Each petal can be pulled individually.
