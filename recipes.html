Gourmet meal is any food of high quality and/or rarity, crafted to deliver exquisite taste and presented in a pleasing fashion.

Flower pizza

This is a recipe that is extremely versatile, it would satisfy everyone, from the pickiest to the biggest gourmets. Feeds: 4-6 people.

Some of the ingredients we would recommend to try on it are: peppers, onions, mushrooms, olives, bacon, prosciutto, mozzarella, cheddar, feta, gorgonzola, and many more. Feel free to make it your own.

Ingredients:

  • Dough:
  • 600g flour
  • 2 large eggs
  • 200ml yogurt
  • 1/2 tsp. sugar
  • 40g fresh yeast
  • 200ml warm milk
  • 1 tsp. baking powder
  • 40g butter / olive oil
  • Fillings:
  • 500g cheese
  • 30g parmigiano
  • 500ml tomato sauce
  • 250g ham / pepperoni
  1. In a small bowl, combine warm milk, sugar, and fresh yeast. Leave it aside to rise.
  2. In the meantime, sift the flour and baking powder into a large bowl. Add a pinch of salt.
  3. Once the yeast mixture has risen, add melted butter (or olive oil), eggs, yogurt, and the yeast mixture into the flour. Mix and knead the dough thoroughly until it becomes compact and smooth. If the dough is sticky, you can gradually add more flour.
  4. Cover the dough and let it rest in a warm spot for an hour or until it roughly doubles in size.
  5. After the dough has risen, transfer it onto a clean surface. Use a rolling pin to stretch the dough to a thickness of about 0.5cm.
  6. Take a large round cookie cutter to cut out dough circles. If you don't have a cookie cutter, a regular round glass can be used as an alternative.
  7. Cut as many dough circles as needed, and the remaining dough can be kneaded, rolled out, and cut again.
  8. Take a dough disc in your hand and add tomato sauce, diced ham (or pepperoni), and grated cheese as desired. Fold the disc in half and twist it around itself to create a petal shape.
  9. Place the shaped petals into a round baking tray, starting from the center and adding more petals around the center ones until the tray is filled.
  10. Sprinkle the flower pizza with parmigiano cheese.
  11. Preheat the oven to 180ºC (350ºF) and bake the flower pizza for approximately 25 minutes.
  12. Once the pizza is done, remove it from the oven and let it rest for 5-10 minutes.
  13. Serve the flower-shaped pizza on a large plate or serving platter. Each petal can be pulled individually.

Gourmet burger

This is one of the all time favorite recipes that contains some family secrets, so let's keep it between us. I am sure you will enjoy it. Feeds: 4 people.

You can make this recipe your own and make as many variations and changes with it and it will still be amazing. Let us know which kind of combinations you come up with.

Ingredients:

  • Patties:
  • 250g bacon
  • Black pepper
  • 500g steak mince
  • Secret Sauce:
  • 100g bacon
  • 1 large onion
  • Garnish:
  • Tomato
  • Lettuce
  • 4 Patties
  • 2 Gherkins
  • Sliced cheese
  • Home-made french fries
  1. For the best burgers, go to the butcher’s and ask for some lean beef steak mince and for him to mince some nice smoky bacon.
  2. In a large mixing bowl mix the beef and bacon mince, add a little freshly ground black pepper. There is no need to add salt, as you will get it from bacon.
  3. Divide the meet into 4, with wet hands roll into a ball, then press flat patties roughly 12cm wide. Place in the fridge to chill.
  4. Prepare the Top Secret Bacon Sauce. Finely dice the onions and bacon. Add them to a preheated non-stick pan, sauté until they become caramelised. Remove from heat and transfer into a food processor, blend until combines into a paste.
  5. Halve the buns, slice the gherkins, tomatoes and finely chop the lettuce.
  6. Cook the burgers two at a time to achieve perfection. Heat up a large non-stick pan on high heat.
  7. Oil lightly and put two burgers into the pan, pressing down on them lightly.
  8. After about 2 minutes, flip the burger and leave for another 2 minutes. Add a cheese slice on top of each burger, add a tiny splash of water to the pan and cover with a lid for about 30 seconds to melt the cheese.
  9. At the same time toast the buns in the same pan we sautéed the onion and bacon for the best flavour.
  10. To build a burger, Add a preferred sauce and lettuce to the bottom of a bun, we recommend a combination of a mayo and ketchup, top with a cheesy burger Pattie, tomatoes and gherkins. Before placing the top bun, spread the Secret Bacon Sauce generously over the bun.
  11. Serve with a fresh home made French fries right away, to prevent the bun soaking too much of the juices, or wrap each burger in a greaseproof paper, give it a minute and enjoy the juicy sloppy goodness.

Cream profiteroles

This recipe is one of our long lasting go to recipes for any type of family gathering. It brings smile onto a face of even the toughest of hearts.

You can make this recipe as it is, or you can elevate it even furhter by adding some fresh fruits, like berries or peaches, nuts, chocolate or cacao nips for little bit of crunch or some herbs, like mint for some freshness.

Ingredients:

  • Profiteroles:
  • 4 eggs
  • 120g flour
  • 100g butter
  • 150ml water
  • pinch of salt
  • 1 tsp. Baking powder
  • Cream:
  • 1 egg
  • 80g flour
  • 140g sugar
  • 500ml milk
  • 150g butter
  • 3 tsp. vanilla sugar
  1. Profiteroles: In a medium-sized pot, combine water and butter, and heat over medium heat until the butter has melted and the mixture starts simmering.
  2. Slowly add the flour mixed with baking powder and salt to the pot, while continuously stirring. Mix for about 10 minutes until the dough forms a smooth consistency, ensuring it doesn't stick to the bottom of the pot.
  3. Remove the pot from heat and gradually incorporate one egg at a time into the dough, making sure each egg is thoroughly combined before adding the next one.
  4. Line a baking tray with parchment paper. Using a teaspoon, scoop half a spoonful of the dough and place it onto the tray, using your finger to gently release it from the spoon. Leaving enough space between each profiterole on the tray to allow for expansion during baking.
  5. Preheat the oven to 180ºC (350ºF) and bake the profiteroles for 30-40 minutes until they turn golden brown. Once out of the oven, immediately cut the profiteroles in half.
  6. Cream: In a pot, heat 400ml of milk until it reaches a simmer. In a separate bowl, mix 100ml of milk with flour and an egg until well combined.
  7. Gradually add the milk mixture to the warm milk in the pot, stirring continuously. Add the sugar to the pot and cook the mixture, stirring consistently, until it thickens.
  8. Remove the pot from heat and allow the cream mixture to cool. In a separate bowl, combine the butter with the vanilla sugar, then incorporate it into the cooled cream mixture.
  9. Assembly:Take the profiteroles and fill them with the prepared cream. Before serving, dust the profiteroles with icing sugar or cover with chocolate for a simple decoration.
